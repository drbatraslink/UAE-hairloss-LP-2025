FAQs

Who can I invite?

You can invite anyone, friends, family, or colleagues who may benefit from hair, skin, or chronic health treatments offered at Dr Batra’s®

What benefits will they receive?

They get a free consultation, full health check-up, blood test as per condition, and a personalised treatment plan by expert doctors.

Do I get any benefits, too?

Yes! You receive Rs 1000 off on Dr Batra’s® products, a 30% discount on treatments, and exclusive wellness benefits when your invitee begins treatment.

What makes the MindFIT program unique?

This program stands out because it empowers you to guide someone you care about toward better health with confidence and expert care.

Is this program open to everyone?

Yes, it is open to all existing patients who want to support a friend or family member’s healing journey through Dr Batra’s®.

How do I share my loved one’s details?

You can walk into any clinic and share the name of the person you would like to help or fill up the form here (hyperlink the form). They will receive a call from our sales executive, who will guide them through the process. No paperwork or long processes are required.

What health issues does Dr Batra’s® treat?

We treat hair fall, dandruff, acne, psoriasis, thyroid, respiratory issues, and many chronic problems with advanced, natural and safe treatments.

What happens after I share the details?

Once your loved one starts treatment, we activate your benefits and offer free treatment to someone who cannot afford care.

Is the first consultation free?

Yes, it includes a thorough consultation, assessment, and a blood test depending on their health concern, with zero charges for the first consultation.

Can I invite more than one person?

Absolutely! There’s no limit. The more you help, the more lives you touch and the more people get access to healing.
